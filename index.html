The Agentic Architecture

Four layers. One invisible loop.

PulseLoop runs a continuous sense → reason → act → learn cycle, silently in the background of your life.

01 · Sense

Wearable Ingestion

Apple Watch, Fitbit, Garmin, WHOOP — continuous biometric stream via Health APIs.

02 · Reason

Agentic RAG Engine

Multimodal LLM cross-references biometrics against clinical knowledge graphs in real-time.

03 · Act

Micro-Intervention

Hyper-personalised nudge delivered via WhatsApp Business API or iMessage at the perfect moment.

04 · Learn

Adaptive Loop

RLHF-style feedback from user responses continuously refines the personal health model.

Technology

Multimodal RAG
meets clinical precision.

Our proprietary Retrieval-Augmented Generation pipeline ingests heterogeneous biometric signals — heart rate variability, sleep architecture, continuous glucose, cortisol proxies — and grounds them against a curated clinical knowledge graph to generate interventions that are medically defensible, not just statistically plausible.

Temporal Reasoning

Understands circadian rhythms and longitudinal trends, not just point-in-time snapshots.

Causal Inference

Correlates lifestyle events (late meetings, travel, diet) with downstream biometric impact.

Privacy-First

Differential privacy, federated learning architecture — your data never leaves your device raw.

Explainability

Every nudge cites its evidence chain — no black-box health advice.
